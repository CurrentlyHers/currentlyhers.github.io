About

Currently Hers is a youth-led nonprofit organization raising awareness of current issues affecting marginalized genders and empowering the leaders of tomorrow.


Subscribe!

Be the first to hear about new updates and opportunities!


Our Work

We use social media to promote voices of people from marginalized genders and raise awareness of gender disparities. We advocate for gender policy reform and produce media to educate youth and bridge to gender gap.


Get Involved

Join a team of high school activists who are pasionate about bridging the gender gap. We are looking for people willing to dedicate time and enthusiasm to our organization, regardless of skill or prior experience. See below for current position openings and don't hesitate to email us at currentlyhers@gmail.com with questions.

Note: Positions are volunteer without compensation. Hour commitment ranges from 1-3 hours per week.

Open Positions

   Writer

Writers are responsible for researching and writing articles for our informative content. Be prepared to write for social media posts, blog articles, and more! Writers communicate regularly with editors to implement feedback and make revisions.

Responsibilities

  • Research and write one Instagram post per week for Currently Hers' Instagram
  • Communicate with editors to implement feedback and make revisions
  • Maintain originality of work and cite sources. No plagiarism.
  • Submit completed work via Google Docs

Skills and Qualifications

  • Organized and able to meet deadlines
  • Advanced vocabulary and grammar knowledge
  • Interest and experience in writing and research

   Editor

Editors plan, review, and revise content produced by writers for publication.

Responsibilities

  • Read content and correct spelling, punctuation, and grammatical errors
  • Rewrite text to improve readability
  • Suggest revisions, such as changing words and rearranging sentences and paragraphs to improve clarity or accuracy
  • Verify facts cited in material for publication and check for plagiarism
  • Work with writers to help their ideas and stories succeed

Skills and Qualifications

  • Detail-oriented to ensure material is error-free
  • Strong language skills and good knowledge of grammar and punctuation rules
  • Able to express ideas clearly and logically
  • Interest and experience in writing

   Artist

Artists work closely with layout designers to illustrate graphics for social media posts and other media.

Responsibilities

  • Determine how best to represent a concept visually
  • Use digital illustration software to create illustrations and help deliver a message
  • Send rough illustrations of design ideas, either by hand sketching or a computer program to layout designers for feedback
  • Incorporate changes recommended by layout designers into final designs

Skills and Qualifications

  • Digital art experience, preferably 1+ years
  • Creative and able to develop unique designs that convey a recognizable meaning
  • Time-management skills to complete illustrations before a deadline

   Layout Designer

Layout designers work closely with artists to create eye-catching page compositions for social media posts and other media. They ensure page text and images are balanced and flow naturally for the reader to follow.

Responsibilities

  • Design layouts that allocate space for the text, photos, and illustrations that make up a story or content
  • Arrange page elements for various media
  • Communicate illustration concepts with artists
  • Assemble layout according to the topic of written content and Currently Hers' theme

Skills and Qualifications

  • Proficiency with Canva or willingness to learn
  • Composition and visual design skills
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Interest and experience in graphic or layout design
